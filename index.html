We cater for the needs of parents who desire excellent educational experience for their children. We are independent co-educational group of schools for children aged 6 months to 10 years and above accommodating both day and boarding learners particularly from age 10 years. The group comprises of Elementary ( Ivory Private Nursery and Primary Schools ) and Secondary ( Ivory Dominion College ). We offer a broad and balanced education within a friendly, caring and happy environment.

At Ivory Private Schools, the curriculum offered is based on the English national curriculum with good reference made to indigenous learning experience. In addition, we have created a strong base for future development in our educational approach. Our Primary Pupils are exposed to fundamental knowledge and essential life skills while our College Students are groomed as global development compliant leaders to take charge of the world tomorrow. This encourages a culture of independent learning that challenges students to accept greater responsibility for their learning.
