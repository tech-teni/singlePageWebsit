Welcome to Diamond Sangard School

Diamond Sangard School is an inspiration from God in memory of late Prophetess Diamond James Warmate, whose life was devoted to the services of mankind and the total of 30 pupils was admitted to start up the school activities. The normal academic classes started on the 9th of September 2017 with sixteen staff, four buildings in well secured environment with fence, playground, sickbay, functional computer in each classroom, kitchen for home Economics, creative Arts studio, Generator house with solar power for constant electricity, Library, independent borehole for steady water supply, security house and functional school wi-fi for internet connectivity.

The Diamond Sangard School was founded in 2017 in memory of late Prophetess Diamond to provide an affordable education that enables it's pupils to be accepted to world renowned universities, colleges, and institution and to contribute to the Nigerian secondary school community to meet the ever growing demand for an internationally recognized education with minimal fees. Our founders and individuals involved in this Diamond Sangard (project) have an extensive and varying experience of service to mankind and a unique dedication to the mission. The Board members, as well as the employees constitute a dedicated internal commitment to the project that is it's core strength and it's forward motion.
